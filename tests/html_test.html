About

Genius Annotation

“心音 (HEART BEAT)” is a song by the J-Pop duo YOASOBI that served as the theme song for the YOASOBI 18祭 that took place on November 19, 2023, and was broadcasted on NHK総合 on the December 25, 2023.

18祭 (18 Fes) is a yearly NHK-sponsored event where a thousand Japanese 18 year-olds collaborate with artists to transform their prospects and worries about the future into a single musical piece performed by both groups.

This backdrop inspired YOASOBI to create “心音 (HEART BEAT)” as it sings about the perseverance of these young adults, leaving behind the troubles of the past and embarking on a new journey for a bright future as they enter the next chapter of life.

Genius Answer

YOASOBI

18歳世代の皆さんが、一人ひとりのHEART BEATと向き合い、大きな声で歌ってくれた歌は、凄まじい熱量とエネルギーに溢れていました。
その歌声が心に響いて、私たちも沢山の事を教えて貰いました。
18歳世代に限らず、様々な世代の人の心に響く「HEART BEAT」になっていると思います。是非放送を楽しみにしていてください。

—via THE FIRST TIMES Official Site
